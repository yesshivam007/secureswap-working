We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register on the SecureSwap, express an interest in obtaining information about us or our products and Services, when you participate in activities on the SecureSwap or otherwise when you contact us.

The personal information that we collect depends on the context of your interactions with us and the SecureSwap, the choices you make and the products and features you use. The personal information we collect may include the following: Name, Email Address, Phone Number (Optional), PAN Card Number (for KYC), Bank Account Details (Account Number, IFSC Code for payouts).

We use personal information collected via our SecureSwap for a variety of business purposes described below...

  • To send administrative information to you.
  • To fulfill and manage your deals and transactions.
  • To request feedback.
  • To protect our Services.
  • To respond to legal requests and prevent harm.
